The AMC 10/12 is a 25-question, 75-minute test for students up to 10th and 12th grade, respectively. Scoring above approximately a 100 on this exam will qualify you for the AIME.

The AIME is a 15-question, 3-hour test for students in high school who qualified mostly through the AMC 10/12. Scoring by (10 * [AIME score] + [AMC Score]), scoring approximately a 220 on this exam may qualify you for the USAMO. Cutoffs may vary.
